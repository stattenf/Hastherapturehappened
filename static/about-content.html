About This Site

Welcome to "Has The Rapture Happened?" - a historical exploration of rapture predictions and their outcomes.

Our Mission

This site documents the various predictions about the rapture throughout history, examining both the predictions themselves and their ultimate outcomes. We believe in learning from history to better understand the present.

What You'll Find Here

  • Historical Predictions: Detailed accounts of rapture predictions from different time periods
  • Outcomes Analysis: What actually happened when these predictions failed to materialize
  • Current Predictions: Information about ongoing or recent rapture predictions
  • Educational Content: Context and background to help understand these movements

Our Approach

We present information objectively, allowing readers to draw their own conclusions. Our goal is education and historical documentation, not to promote or discourage any particular religious viewpoint.
